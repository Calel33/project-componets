Frequently asked — questions

Everything you need to know about integrating NOVA Chat.

How long does integration take?

Most teams ship a production chat in under a week using our SDKs and templates. Web, iOS, and Android are covered, plus REST and WebSocket APIs.

Is end‑to‑end encryption enabled by default?

Yes for direct messages and optional per‑channel for group chats. Key rotation and device verification are provided out of the box.

Can we migrate from an existing provider?

We offer assisted migrations with message import, user mapping, and zero‑downtime cutovers for Pro and Enterprise plans.

What about rate limits and scale?

Generous defaults with burst capacity. Enterprise plans include dedicated regions, custom limits, and performance SLAs.

Where can I find examples?

See our starter kits and sample apps in the docs. We also provide prebuilt chat UIs you can theme in minutes.
